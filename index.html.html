Resident portal

Everything a resident needs, minus the front-desk queue

Six connected modules cover the moments residents actually deal with day to day.

Wallet & rent payments

Top up, pay rent, and download receipts — with a full transaction history on demand.

My room & lease

Room type, amenities, and lease dates in one place — plus roommate details.

Guest invitations

Invite a visitor, set their date, and let the front desk see it coming — no calls needed.

Campus services

Borrow equipment, book a laundry slot, and check the next shuttle — all in one tab.

Maintenance & complaints

Log a fault or report damage, then track it through to resolved — no chasing the office.

Notices & community

Property announcements plus quick links to the Adana socials residents already follow.

Two sides, one platform

Built for residents day to day — and staff every month-end

For residents

A self-service home base

  • See rent and wallet balance the moment you log in
  • Book laundry and equipment without walking to the office
  • Track a maintenance ticket from "submitted" to "resolved"
  • Invite guests in advance, on your own time
For Adana Student Pods staff

A control room for the property

  • See who's paid, who's pending, who's overdue — at a glance
  • Automate rent reminders and receipt generation
  • Assign and resolve tickets without losing the thread
  • Keep one directory of every resident on the property
